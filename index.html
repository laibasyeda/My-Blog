My Journey in Web Development

From the early days of learning HTML, my journey has been nothing short of exhilarating. Each challenge has been an opportunity to grow and evolve.

Structural and Semantic markup play a crucial role in the web development process. Structural markup, achieved through HTML tags like <div>, <section>, and <article>, helps organize and structure content on a page.

  • Structural markup is the blueprint of a website, defining the layout and hierarchy of information.

  • On the other hand, Semantic markup, such as using <h1> for headings, <p> for paragraphs, and <strong> for strong importance, adds meaning and context to the content.

  • Semantic markup enhances accessibility and SEO, making the content more meaningful for both users and search engines.
